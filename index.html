wead.ai

Vision & Identity

wead.ai is a digital forensics and memory infrastructure platform created to help individuals make sense of disordered personal and legal archives. Born from a fight against HOA corruption, it has evolved into a broader tool for extracting order from chaos — spanning timelines, ancestry, legal documentation and personal storytelling.

Use Cases & Features

  • Legal timeline generation: detect events, violations, dates and actors from document archives.
  • Ancestry & legacy archiving: organize family records into structured timelines.
  • Medical/health chronologies: track histories across scans, diagnoses and treatments.
  • Paper hoard triage: convert unstructured folders into tagged, indexed and searchable archives.
  • HOA & foreclosure pattern detection: connect financial documents, board minutes and tax or lien data for repeat abuse.

Method Highlights

wead.ai employs a modular AI-driven workflow to transform large volumes of disparate records into searchable, indexed archives and generate human-readable chronological narratives. This includes ingestion, OCR and text classification, metadata alignment, temporal anchoring, pattern detection, cross‑reference linking and narrative output generation.

  • Pluggable analyzers and deep OCR for text classification.
  • Regulatory pattern libraries to flag potential RICO, FIRREA, HUD, FHA and ADA violations.
  • Timeline builder and violation detection engine.
  • Folder auto‑tagging with export to Notion/JSON.

Our Story

In 2022, a box of heirlooms containing family letters, wills, deeds and medical records inspired the creation of wead.ai. Since then, the project has grown into a system that blends legal retaliation recovery with ancestral restoration and investigative intelligence — built not for profit, but for purpose.
